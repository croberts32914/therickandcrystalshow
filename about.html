      Sometimes we want to give up, but we learn to pick ourselves up from the ground, wipe our tears and try again. Although it may be comfortable to remain in the bubble you were born into, the role you were expected to fulfill; you will undoubtedly find that role unsatisfying as this was not the role, in your heart which you believed was your purpose.

We are ALL human beings sharing a human experience.
Everyone has a story and that story matters, because we matter, every last one of us!


    Like everyone, Rick and Crystal have a story... Their story triumphs with God, love, understanding, forgiveness and a desire to help others in need, but that’s not how it first started...

      Rick and Crystal have struggled with many tragic and stressful situations in their lifetimes. Some things that have happened they never thought they would emotionally recover from. One of the most tragic, life changing events, that happened in Rick's life was when he lost his spouse of 22 years. He was a widower of 3 boys when he met Crystal in 2013. was a divorced mother of two boys often in court for various custody issues. They have survived sexual abuse, physical abuse and attempted suicides. Both Rick and Crystal recovered from past drug and alcohol abuse. Since the beginning of Rick and Crystals' relationship they have had loss of loved ones from age; health; suicide; accidental OD; and murder. Although they have felt loss and at times desperation from overwhelming feelings from situations, they wanted, they needed, what every other person desires: to find peace, happiness, joy, and security.

Crystal's struggles with her bipolar diagnosis, and accepting the views of self from others perspective. She's in search of self after being told almost her entire life who she is, what she thinks and even what she herself believes.


Romans 3:23, in the Bible says "For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God".

      We all have our own personal cross to bear, as they say and Rick and Crystal are no exception. They both have ultimately made some bad decisions in their past which not only affected their present but future as well.

      Their sins were overcoming them, they were drowning, sinking but grasping for a moment of peace, just a moment would be nice they both had thought. Lying to themselves, hiding their pain, falling into the socially acceptable, lure of alcohol and its lifestyle until Rick had been drinking one night and got a DUI and lost his license, his freedom, his career, and truck, what had appeared to be almost everything. But something unexpected happened...

      Rick and Crystal began not only seeking truth and understanding, of who they were and why they had become the people they had; but they wanted to improve their quality of life, someway, somehow. They believed they could naturally alter their perception of reality by gaining knowledge and understanding of things such as: self, society, politics and creation. Before then, up until that bad day, they unknowingly were existing in a slumber, but until someone is aware there is a problem, there will never be a solution. For Rick and Crystal, that bad night had a good outcome... because it saved them, their marriage and their family. That night saved their LIVES!


Rick and Crystal's story’s not over just yet, it’s still being written!

Rick and Crystal are not the same people they once were and for that they are GREATFUL!!!
